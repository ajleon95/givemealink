give me a . link!

givemea.link is a website that selects a random link for you, the user. The links are currently curated from the subreddit /r/InternetIsBeautiful, but may be expanded in the future to include other subreddits or even user submissions.

givemea.link uses the Fat-Free Framework for the back-end and templating, Bourbon, et al. for styles, and the jQuery library for DOM manipulation.
